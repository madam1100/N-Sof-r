The Taste of Albanian Tradition

In the heart of the city is the restaurant "Në Sofër", a place where Albanian hospitality and tradition come together perfectly. Each dish is prepared with love, using fresh ingredients and authentic recipes from our traditional cuisine. The warm atmosphere and attentive service make every visit an unforgettable experience.

" Në Sofër, every dish is a journey into the heart of Albanian tradition, where authentic flavors and warm hospitality create an experience that lingers in the mind and heart. "

The Taste of the Royal Table

An elegant combination of slow-roasted beef, accompanied by truffle mashed potatoes and vegetables caramelized in aromatic butter. Every element in this dish is intended to bring to the table the rich flavor of Albanian tradition with a modern and regal touch.

" In every dish at Në Sofër, there is an old song hidden, which sings to your taste and conquers your soul. "

The Secret of the Warm Sofra

At Në Sofër, pasta is not just a meal – it is a refined experience between Mediterranean flavor and artisanal care. Cooked fresh every day, they are accompanied by sauces selected from local ingredients: sun-ripened tomatoes, aromatic basil and expertly seasoned cheese.

" At Në Sofër, every dish comes with flavors cooked with heart, where Albanian tradition meets modern care to give every visitor the feeling of an unforgettable meal. "

Desserts That Speak to the Heart

At the end of every meal at Në Sofër, a magical world of desserts awaits you that doesn’t just end with taste. Each dessert is crafted with dedication, from nutty baklava and warm sorbet, to creme brulée with an Albanian twist. With fresh ingredients and elegant presentation, our desserts are the final embrace that gives meals a sweet and unforgettable ending.
